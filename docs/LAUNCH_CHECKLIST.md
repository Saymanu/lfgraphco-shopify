# L F Graphco Launch Checklist

Review date: 2026-06-13

This checklist is for the quote-first Shopify Online Store 2.0 theme with a secondary ready-to-order retail apparel channel.

## Launch Priorities

- Keep custom apparel, promotional products, banners, signs, quote requests and phone calls as the primary business path.
- Use Shop Collections as a secondary ecommerce path for simple retail shirt designs.
- Position full-color apparel production as a decoration method, not as a standalone transfer supplier business.
- Confirm all forms, calls-to-action and navigation paths support quote requests, artwork upload, contact sales, add to cart and checkout.

## Required Before Publishing

1. Upload the theme to Shopify and preview desktop and mobile views.
2. Create Shopify pages with matching handles for service, quote, upload, contact, FAQ, local SEO, banners/signs and Shop Collections templates.
3. Create collections for custom quote categories and the five retail collections:
   - Bridal Party Shirts
   - Grandma / Nana / Mimi Shirts
   - Sports Mom Shirts
   - Birthday and Family Event Shirts
   - Texas Pride Shirts
4. Add retail products with product images, size variants, shirt color variants, prices and optional personalization fields.
5. Add or confirm quote-category collections for Custom T-Shirts, Embroidered Polos, Hoodies, Safety Apparel, Company Uniforms, Spirit Wear, Banners and Signs and Promotional Products.
6. Test Contact, Request A Quote, Upload Artwork, homepage lead capture and exit-intent forms.
7. Confirm the sticky mobile CTA calls `972-840-8337` and points to Request A Quote.
8. Configure payment, checkout, tax, shipping, pickup/delivery, notification emails and store policies.
9. Add final Shopify SEO titles and meta descriptions for every live page.
10. Enter tracking IDs only after GA4, GTM, Meta Pixel, Klaviyo and Google Ads accounts are ready.

## Required Before Google Ads

- Verify GA4 page views.
- Verify GTM container rendering.
- Configure Google Ads conversion actions for quote leads, contact leads, artwork submissions, phone clicks and purchases.
- Verify Search Console and submit the Shopify sitemap.
- Confirm ad landing pages do not over-emphasize DTF transfer sales.
- Add privacy/cookie disclosure language for analytics, retargeting and email marketing.

## Required Before Merchant Center

- Add feed-ready retail products with images, prices, availability and shipping settings.
- Keep quote-only custom products clearly labeled or out of shopping feeds unless they have fixed purchasable pricing.
- Connect Shopify to Google Merchant Center using the Google & YouTube app or approved feed app.
- Resolve feed diagnostics before scaling Shopping or Performance Max campaigns.

## Remaining User Decisions

- Whether to launch with artwork link fields or connect a true file upload app.
- Whether mobile navigation should remain horizontally scrollable or become a collapsible menu.
- Which retail designs, mockups, prices and personalization rules should launch first.
- Which customer testimonials or review proof can be published.
