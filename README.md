# L F Graphco Shopify Storefront

Production-ready Shopify Online Store 2.0 theme for L F Graphco LLC in Garland, Texas.

## Business focus

L F Graphco LLC provides screen printing, embroidery, DTF printing, promotional products, company uniforms, school spirit wear, team stores and fundraisers. The storefront is built to generate quote requests and ecommerce sales for schools, booster clubs, churches, construction companies, restaurants, medical offices, small businesses and sports teams.

## Included

- Mobile-first Shopify theme scaffold
- Homepage and required service pages
- Contact, request quote, upload artwork and FAQ templates
- Blog, product, collection and cart templates
- SEO landing page templates for Garland, Mesquite, Dallas and DFW
- Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, Klaviyo and Google Ads placeholders
- Merchant Center, deployment and tracking documentation

## Theme settings

Configure the business phone, location, primary CTA, brand colors and marketing IDs in Shopify theme settings. Tracking snippets stay inactive until IDs are entered.
