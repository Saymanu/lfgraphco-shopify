# Project Strategy

## Positioning

L F Graphco LLC is primarily a custom apparel, promotional products, banners and signs company serving schools, booster clubs, churches, sports teams, contractors, restaurants, medical offices, municipalities and small businesses.

The website should generate quote requests first and ecommerce sales second.

## Revenue Mix

- 80% custom printing, apparel programs, promotional products, banners, signs, artwork upload and sales conversations.
- 20% ready-to-order retail apparel collections that customers can buy without a quote.

## Core Customer-Facing Services

- Screen Printing
- Embroidery
- Promotional Products
- Company Uniform Programs
- School Spirit Wear
- Team Stores
- Fundraisers
- Banners and Signs

## DTF / Full-Color Production Positioning

DTF is an internal production method that L F Graphco may use for detailed full-color apparel projects. The site should not position L F Graphco as a standalone DTF transfer supplier.

Customer-facing language should favor:

- Full-color apparel printing
- Detailed artwork production
- Full-color custom apparel

Avoid leading with:

- DTF transfer supplier
- Gang sheet seller
- Transfer-only storefront

## Retail Shop Collections

Ready-to-order retail collections should be simple, high-margin and easy to produce:

- Bridal Party Shirts
- Grandma / Nana / Mimi Shirts
- Sports Mom Shirts
- Birthday and Family Event Shirts
- Texas Pride Shirts

Retail customers should be able to select a design, size, shirt color, optional personalization, add to cart, checkout and ship.

## Homepage Strategy

The homepage should follow this priority:

1. Hero section with Request Quote CTA.
2. Core services section.
3. Featured Shop Collections section.
4. Why Choose L F Graphco.
5. Reviews and testimonials.
6. Upload Artwork CTA.
7. Contact / lead capture section.

Additional proof, pricing and trust sections may remain below the primary conversion path as long as they do not dilute the quote-first strategy.
